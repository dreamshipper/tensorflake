About Me

Raised in Rajshahi, Bangladesh, I hold an MBA degree and BBA in Finance & Banking from the University of Rajshahi. My professional journey began in IT as a MIS Associate, where I discovered the power of data in driving business decisions. That passion for data and systems led me to aviation—an industry where I could blend technology with strategy, and where I found my true calling. Today, I bring over 12 years plus of progressive experience in airline commercial operations, spanning both full-service and low-cost carriers. My expertise includes Sales & Marketing, Route Development, Pricing, Revenue Management, Distribution, GSA Management, and Market Strategy—all supported by a data-driven mindset and hands-on leadership.
